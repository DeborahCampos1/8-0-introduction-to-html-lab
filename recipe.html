The Perfect Stir Fry

Description

Stir Frys are some of the easiest and potetentially healthiest meals.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs of Sesame or Olive oil
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Tbs of Tamari or Soy sauce
  • Whatever veggies you like!

Directions

  1. Heat oil and saute garlic for 1 minute.
  2. Add veggies, cook for three minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Add Tamari or soy sauce, cook for 1 more minute stirring frequently.
  4. Serve over rice
